Exercise 3: What are the differences between HTML and HTML 5?

In HTML5 the users cookies are stored locally on the user's browser. Audio and video playback no longer require additional plug-ins. You don't need Adobe Flash to play videos like in the old days.

HTML5 gives the developer more control over the website site. HTML5 is more mobile support friendly. HTML5 allows drag and drop features.

Simon Francis Mann (born 26 June 1952) is a British former Army officer and mercenary. Mann trained to be an officer at Sandhurst and was commissioned into the Scots Guards, he later became a member of the SAS. On leaving the military, he co-founded the private military company, Executive Outcomes in 1993[1] and then co-founded Sandline International with fellow ex-Scots Guards Colonel Tim Spicer in 1996.
