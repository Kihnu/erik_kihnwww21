Exercise 3: What are the differences between HTML and HTML 5?

In HTML5 the users cookies are stored locally on the user's browser. Audio and video playback no longer require additional plug-ins. You don't need Adobe Flash to play videos like in the old days.

HTML5 gives the developer more control over the website site. HTML5 is more mobile support friendly. HTML5 allows drag and drop features.
